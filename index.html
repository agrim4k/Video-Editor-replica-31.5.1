Neural Editor Manual

Import

Use the Import button to add videos or images. Use Audio -> Import Custom for music.

Timeline

Drag clips from the library to the timeline. Drag the center of a clip to move it, or the edges to trim it.

Effects

Select a clip, go to the Effects tab, and click an FX card to apply neural transforms (Zoom In/Out) or filters.

Export

Click Launch Export to render your composition into a WebM neural stream.
